GWC Robot Week Day 1

July 10, 2017

Today we talked a lot about robots and what characteristics define robots. We defined robots as anything that is programmed to do a certain task. My idea of robots changed because now I know that they are more than just a Roomba (self-vacuuming vacuum). I am excited about the future of robots because they can really change the way humans do things and they have great potential to help disabled people in many ways. We researched about Kavita Krishnaswamy and all her work in robots to help disabled people move, which is super cool! However, it does make me a little scared and nervous because robots can become smarter than humans (but we have a while until then!).

We also worked in groups on circuits today. I think it was great because most of my group members were familiar with what we were doing an really helped me out. Next time, we could move closer together and work because the big table was a bit awkward. We were far from each other so it was difficult for us to all work at the same time.

We talked about two amazing women who work in the field of robotics today: Manuela Veloso and Ayanna Howard. They both design and build robots to do different tasks and make people's lives easier and better.

My team worked with the Arduino and coded a lightshow. We split up the work into wiring and coding and made sure that all of us understood what was going on. The basic pieces of every Arduino sketch/code is the setup in the beginning and the loop for the actual lightshow.
